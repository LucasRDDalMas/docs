# PARA Method

**Source:** Tiago Forte, Forte Labs  
**Type:** article  

## Summary

PARA is a universal system for organising digital information, designed to be action-oriented
rather than topic-oriented.

## Key Points

- **Projects** — series of tasks with a goal and deadline
- **Areas** — spheres of activity with a standard to maintain over time
- **Resources** — topics or interests that may be useful in the future
- **Archive** — inactive items from the other three categories

The critical insight is to organise by *actionability*, not by *topic*.

## Quotes

> "The goal of a knowledge management system isn't to preserve information, it's to
> make you more effective at your work."
